EVENT 1

INDUCTIONS

25-08-2023

The CYBERNAUTS induction event welcomed new members into our dynamic coding community, celebrating innovation, collaboration, and a passion for technology. Highlights included interactive workshops and inspiring talks from industry leaders.

EVENT 2

INTRODUCTORY SESSIONS

05-09-2023

Club, recently conducted its introductory session. Attendees were introduced to the club's mission, upcoming projects, and the exciting opportunities that lie ahead. This session marks the beginning of our collaborative journey in the world of coding and technology.

EVENT 3

WEB-D WORKSHOP

21-10-2023

An engaging Web Development (WebD) workshop where students were introduced to the basics of web development, including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Participants also completed hands-on projects, creating their own web pages and applications.

EVENT 4

DSA (Data Structures & Algorithms)

25-12-2023

Workshop on Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA) held . Attendees were introduced to core concepts and learned how to effectively navigate through various data structures and algorithms. This workshop provided a solid foundation for understanding and applying DSA in real-world scenarios.

EVENT 5

MACHINE LEARNING & AI/ML

15-06-2024

Club recently hosted a captivating event on Machine Learning and AI/ML fundamentals. Attendees explored key concepts, essential algorithms, and real-world applications. This inspiring session has beautifully equipped participants to dive deeper into the fascinating world of AI.
